## About

K-BAP is a South African Korean food company built around real food and careful hosting.

The brand started with catering and grew through repeat events, cultural tables, and people asking where they could buy the food again.

Our food is loved by Korean guests who know the reference point and by South African guests discovering Korean food properly.

## FAQ Copy

### Do you cater corporate and cultural events?

Yes. K-BAP caters corporate meals, cultural events, festivals, private gatherings, and institutional tables.

### Can non-Korean guests enjoy the food?

Yes. The food stays authentic, but the menu is easy to understand. Heat levels and familiar entry points can be planned for each event.

### Is the menu only available as a PDF?

No. The website menu is built in HTML so people can browse dishes, categories, and catering options directly.

### Will K-BAP sell products online?

Yes. The website is prepared for K-BAP Kimchi, meal kits, sauces, frozen products, and market drops.
