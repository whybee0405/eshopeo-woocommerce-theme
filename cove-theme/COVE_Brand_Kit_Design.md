# COVE Appliances — Brand Kit & Design Guidelines

## Brand Overview

**COVE** is a clean, modern online appliances brand selling new, demo, and graded appliances from trusted major brands such as LG, Samsung, Hisense, Bosch, Defy, and more.

The brand should feel premium, clear, calm, and trustworthy — inspired by Apple-like minimalism with a soft touch of liquid morphism.

**Core positioning:**  
Premium appliances. Clearly graded. Better priced.

**Primary tagline:**  
Appliances made clear.

---

## Brand Essence

### 1. Premium Home Technology
Modern, reliable appliances from leading brands.

### 2. Transparent Grading
Clear New, A, B, and C grade systems so customers know exactly what they are buying.

### 3. Smart Value
Exceptional value without compromising quality or trust.

### 4. Simple Online Buying
Easy browsing, clear information, and a seamless shopping experience.

### 5. Trusted Brands
Products from appliance brands customers already know and trust.

---

## Logo System

### Primary Wordmark
`COVE`

The main logo should be uppercase, spacious, minimal, and premium.

### Primary Lockup
`COVE APPLIANCES`

Used for the website header, packaging, official documents, invoices, and brand collateral.

### Brand Mark / Icon
The **O** in COVE becomes the brand mark.

Design direction:
- Soft rounded square/circle hybrid
- Glass-like or liquid edge treatment
- Light blue gradient or reflective finish
- Inspired by appliance screens, smart home buttons, and liquid glass UI

### Logo Variations Required
- Primary wordmark
- Primary lockup
- Compact wordmark
- Brand mark only
- Monochrome dark
- Monochrome light
- Inverted version
- Stacked lockup
- Grade badge lockups

## Visual Direction

COVE should feel like a premium digital showroom, not a discount warehouse.

### Visual Keywords
- Clean
- Premium
- Minimal
- Bright
- Clear
- Trustworthy
- Soft-tech
- Liquid glass
- Apple-like
- Practical luxury

### Liquid Morphism Usage
Use subtle liquid glass effects in:
- Hero backgrounds
- Brand icon
- Product cards
- CTA panels
- Grade badges
- Feature cards

Avoid:
- Heavy neon effects
- Crypto-style blobs
- Overly futuristic UI
- Excessive blur
- Dark cyberpunk visuals

---

## Photography Direction

### Product Photography
Clean product cut-outs on white, porcelain, or soft gradient backgrounds.

Focus on:
- Premium finishes
- Clear product shape
- Minimal shadows
- Honest product representation

### Lifestyle Photography
Use modern, bright South African-friendly home environments.

Ideal scenes:
- Clean kitchens
- Laundry rooms
- Living spaces
- Minimal interiors
- Natural light

### Detail Photography
Use close-ups of:
- Appliance display panels
- Stainless steel textures
- Handles
- Digital controls
- Energy labels
- Cosmetic condition marks

For graded/demo appliances, photography must feel honest and reassuring.

---

## Grade System

### New
Factory sealed or brand-new condition.

### A Grade
Excellent condition. Minimal to no visible marks.

### B Grade
Light cosmetic marks. Fully functional.

### C Grade
Visible cosmetic wear. Tested and functional.

The grade system must always feel trustworthy and professional, not like a warning label.

## Product Card Requirements

Each product card should clearly show:
- Product image
- Brand name
- Model number
- Product category
- Grade
- Price
- Was price / saving amount
- Delivery availability
- Warranty or inspection badge

### Product Card Tone
Clean, premium, and easy to scan.

## Final Creative Direction

COVE should feel like:

> A premium online appliance showroom for smart buyers who want trusted brands, clear condition grading, and better value.

It should not feel like a discount appliance warehouse or second-hand marketplace.

The emotional takeaway should be:

> “I’m getting a great deal, but from a brand that feels professional, clear, and trustworthy.”
